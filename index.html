Canon 1200D

The Canon EOS 1200D is designed to be the new entry level Digital SLR from Canon. Featuring an 18 million-pixel sensor. This camera is mostly used during movies and studio shooting.

canon 18-55mm

The Canon EOS 18-55mm is the new standard lens for Canon DSLR cameras. 4-Stop the optical image stabilization and has a nice general purpose focal length range.

canon 55-250mm

The Canon EOS 55-250mm Lens is a small, light and economical telephoto zoom with a long focal length range - one that matches up very well to many general purpose zoom lenses.

protect filter

The Canon 55mm Protect Filter is a general use, clear filter that helps to guard your lens from dust, scratches, moisture and other elements. It is ideal for leaving on a lens at all times.
